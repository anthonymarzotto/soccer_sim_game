VariantB Simulation Tuning Variables

This document tracks all 42 fields of VariantBTuningConfig (src/app/models/simulation.types.ts). Defaults live in DEFAULT_VARIANT_B_TUNING at the top of match.simulation.variant-b.service.ts. Pass a Partial<VariantBTuningConfig> via SimulationConfig.variantBTuning to override at match start; the debug panel exposes all fields at runtime.

Shot: On-Target Gate — first resolution gate; uses shooting
onTargetBaseshot gate 1 0.31 Starting probability before skill and position adjustments. At average shooting (70), this is the on-target rate.
onTargetSkillScaleshot gate 1 0.0045 Scales the shooting − 70 delta. A shooter with 90 shooting adds 0.09 to the base; a 50-rated shooter loses 0.09.
onTargetWidePenaltyshot gate 1 0.06 Deducted proportionally to lateral distance from centre (lateralDistance / 50 × penalty). At full lateral distance, costs up to 6 pp accuracy.
onTargetFatiguePenaltyshot gate 1 0.04 Flat deduction when the shooter's fatigueLevel exceeds 75. Connects in-match PlayerFatigue state to shot accuracy.
onTargetMin / onTargetMaxshot gate 1 0.15 / 0.82 Hard clamp after all modifiers. No shot can be less than 15 % or more than 82 % on-target regardless of skill or fatigue.
Shot: Goal Gate — second gate (only reached for on-target shots); uses shooting vs keeperSkill
goalChanceBaseshot gate 2 0.25 Starting probability before skill, position, and home-advantage adjustments. At parity between shooter and keeper, this is the conversion rate.
goalChanceSkillVsKeeperScaleshot gate 2 0.0033 Scales the shooting − keeperSkill delta. A 20-point shooting advantage adds about 6.6 pp; a 20-point deficit costs the same.
goalChanceWidePenaltyshot gate 2 0.035 Same lateral-distance penalty structure as gate 1 but smaller — wide shots already fail the on-target gate more often, so the double penalty is intentionally lighter.
goalChanceMin / goalChanceMaxshot gate 2 0.10 / 0.55 Hard clamp. An on-target shot always converts at least 10 % of the time; no conversion exceeds 55 % regardless of keeper quality or home advantage.
