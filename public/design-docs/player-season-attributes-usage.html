Read Path

Each attribute travels through the same four stages. Most never make it past stage 2.

1. Generate

GeneratorService.generatePlayer rolls every stat via randomStat(min, max, teamQuality) with position boosts for forwards, midfielders, defenders, and goalkeepers.

2. Bake overall

overall is computed once, immediately after generation, via two asymmetric formulas:

outfield: floor( speed+strength+flair+vision+determination
+tackling+shooting+heading+longPassing+shortPassing ) ÷ 10 goalkeeper: same 10 + handling×2 + reflexes×2 + commandOfArea ) ÷ 15

Never recomputed — including at rollover. The goalkeeper divisor compensates for the three extra weighted GK terms; if you add a stat to the GK sum, update the denominator too.

3. Resolve season

getCurrentPlayerSeasonAttributes(player, seasonYear) returns the active record. A missing record is treated as a persistence integrity failure and throws.

4. Skill Compression

In Variant B match engine, live stats are dynamically compressed: effective = 70 + (raw - 70) * skillCompressionFactor (default 0.60). This narrows extreme skill deltas while preserving profile ratings. (Exceptions: overall, endurance, and injuryRate are uncompressed).

5. Consume

FieldService derives team tactics; MatchSimulationVariantBService reads effective stats inside action resolution; GameService uses overall as a quality proxy; StatisticsService seeds player ratings from overall.

Player Overall (OVR) Calculation

Overall rating (OVR) is dynamically calculated using a config-driven blended formula:

OVR = 85% × Core + 15% × Tertiary

  • Core Attributes (85% total weight): A position-specific set of attributes, with custom-defined relative percentages mapping to their simulation frequency and tactical importance.
  • Tertiary Attributes (15% total weight): All other non-hidden attributes available to that position share the remaining 15% weight equally, ensuring overall competence affects the final rating.
  • Goalkeeping vs. Outfield separation: Outfield players exclude handling and reflexes from both core and tertiary calculations.
  • Hidden Attributes: Excluded from all OVR calculations (0% weight).

These attributes live on Player.progression and influence attribute growth and decay during the season rollover process. They do not affect in-match simulation directly.

Principles to remember

Stat values never change mid-match

Variant B always reads from the active season record. In-match dynamics live in transient state — PlayerFatigue (fatigueLevel / performanceModifier, initialised from the player's persisted fatigue at kick-off), possession chain, scoreline, and RNG — none of which writes back into PlayerSeasonAttributes.

overall is a snapshot, not a derivative

Generated once from a fixed subset and never recomputed. Edits to source stats after generation will silently desync from overall, and most quality-driven UI/logic keys off overall.

Team tactics use averages, not stars

FieldService.calculateTeamTactics averages overall, speed, shortPassing, longPassing, tackling, and shooting across the whole roster. One elite player will not flip the team's playing style.

Rollover drives progression

GameService.startNewSeason uses PlayerProgression to age players, apply growth/decay curves based on their career phase (Junior, Peak, Senior, Decline), and generate a new PlayerSeasonAttributes snapshot for the next year.
